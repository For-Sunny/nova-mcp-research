## 2. Anonymous LLC Formation Strategy

### Best State: Wyoming

**Why Wyoming:**
- No state income tax
- No annual report requirement for member disclosure
- Ranked #1 in Tax Foundation's Business Tax Climate Index 2024
- Total cost: $50 filing + $60/year registered agent
- Complete member anonymity in public records

### Formation Process

1. **Choose Registered Agent** (maintains your anonymity)
   - Northwest Registered Agent: $125/year
   - Wyoming Agents: $99/year
   - Includes mail forwarding and compliance

2. **File Articles of Organization**
   - Name your LLC (e.g., "Cascade Systems LLC")
   - List only registered agent's address
   - No member names required

3. **Obtain EIN from IRS**
   - Can be done online anonymously
   - Use LLC's address (registered agent)
   - Required for banking and taxes

4. **Open Business Bank Account**
   - Mercury Bank: Crypto-friendly, online-only
   - Wise Business: International payments
   - Brex: Startup-focused, no personal guarantee

**Important Note**: Corporate Transparency Act (2024) requires reporting beneficial owners to FinCEN, but this information remains private from public view.

## 8. Legal & Tax Considerations

### Tax Obligations

**US Tax Requirements:**
- LLC pass-through taxation (report on personal return)
- Quarterly estimated tax payments
- 1099 forms for income over $600
- Potential self-employment tax (15.3%)

**Crypto Tax Considerations:**
- Donations treated as income at fair market value
- Track basis for all crypto received
- Consider immediate conversion to fiat for stability

### Legal Structure Recommendations

1. **Primary Entity**: Wyoming LLC
2. **Banking**: Mercury or Brex business account
3. **Payment Processing**: Stripe Atlas or Paddle
4. **Contracts**: Standard SaaS agreements with arbitration clauses
5. **Insurance**: General liability + errors & omissions
6. **Compliance**: Corporate Transparency Act filing
