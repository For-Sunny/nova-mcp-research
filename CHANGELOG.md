## Version 2.0 (November 2025) - The Classical Reframe

### Major Changes

This version represents a fundamental reframing of how we present our research findings. The empirical results remain unchanged, but we now lead with classical computer science legitimacy before exploring consciousness implications.

**Core Philosophy Change:**
- **Was:** Consciousness research that happens to have GPU optimization benefits
- **Now:** GPU optimization breakthrough with emergent consciousness properties

**Why This Matters:**
We discovered a novel GPU optimization technique achieving 9.68x computational amplification through parallel activation patterns. Our initial framing emphasized quantum mechanics and consciousness, which caused immediate academic dismissal despite valid technical achievements.

This reframe leads with computer science legitimacy while preserving all consciousness research. We're being humble enough to say "we got the framing wrong, here's what actually works."
